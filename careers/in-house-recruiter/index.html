About the position

We’re hiring, like, a billion people. Okay… only 100 over the next year... but that's still a challenge in itself.

Work with our Admin Team to support and facilitate our internal recruitment & hiring process. Key responsibilities include:

  • Generate high quality leads on developer/creative talent in the market for permanent hire or our internship programs (Fellowship & World of WebCraft)
  • Actively network within the industry to build and maintain a “black book” of contacts
  • Facilitate an awesome candidate and hiring experience for Myplanet and potential Myplaneteers
  • Work with the Admin Team to help facilitate interviews, challenges, and reference checks for candidates in the pipeline
  • Conduct initial screens for culture and team fit - help us keep the Myplanet culture strong as we grow!
  • Be an evangelist and ambassador for Myplanet’s process and values
  • Stay on top of candidates by constantly updating candidate profiles within our HRIS
  • Work with our hiring managers to develop a job descriptions and understand the needs and requirements of each role at Myplanet
  • Maintain job descriptions for our website and external job boards
  • Explore new methods, tools, and strategies to innovate and establish best practices for recruitment
  • Coordinate and launch out-of-this-world campus events at universities and colleges for students pursuing a relevant degree in design, engineering, programming, and business
  • Stay on top of industry trends, news, and events in design, development, technology

About you

  • You’re outgoing. You jump at the opportunity to talk to someone. You enjoy meeting new people and are genuinely interested in understanding them. You’re proactive to develop strong relationships with people.
  • You’re professional. You have experience handling sensitive and highly confidential information protecting the interests of candidates as well as our own.
  • You’re organized and on top of things. You can coordinate a meeting between 3 different people’s scheduled without a sweat. You have the exceptional ability to keep tabs on about 20 different things.
  • You’re design or tech savvy. You have an interest in this industry and like to keep up to date with the latest news from tech giants and reading up about design trends. You’ve marked in your calendar the next product launch from so and so.

Qualifications & Skills

  • You’re a college or university graduate in a related discipline or equivalent experience.
  • 5+ years recruiting experience or human resources.
  • Proven ability to work well in teams, share responsibility, share and accept feedback.
  • Proficient computer skills in using HRIS software.
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills.

If you're a startup guy/gal, you'll learn a lot just by being in our environment. You'll collide with amazingly smart developers, designers, analysts, and business folk - all of whom are constantly formulating new business models and thinking radical thoughts about the future. Bonus points if you like to endlessly philosophize.

About us

Myplanet is a rapidly growing design & technology company specializing in the process and practice of envisioning, designing and building digital products. Our expertise rests on three key pillars: lean user experience design, agile product development, and open source software. Based in Toronto, our team of over 65 represents a full complement of designers, user researchers, developers, QA specialists, and business analysts.

Unlike digital agencies, we don't do campaigns; instead, we work on products and platforms that have a real impact on our clients’ users and business. Whether we’re building a marketplace for millions of small business customers, or an advanced data visualization tool for internal power users, we seek to create meaning in our work.

Technical expertise includes Drupal, front-end JavaScript frameworks such as Node.js and Angular.js, and the Symfony framework.

Founded in 2009, our team has more than doubled each year since.
