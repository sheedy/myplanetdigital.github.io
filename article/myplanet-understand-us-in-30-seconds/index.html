Understand Myplanet's Purpose in 30 Seconds

Dustin Walper Friday, Jan 17, 2014
  • business

Build the right thing, fast.

Building great digital products isn't easy. That's where we come in: we help organizations rapidly build user-focused web and mobile products. We combine the best aspects of Lean, Agile, and UX design methodologies.

But we don't just build things. We empower organizations to become lean, mean product development machines.

What we do well

There are four things that we do well:

  1. We help you figure out what to build, why, and for whom.
  2. We turn that insight into beautiful web-based and mobile products.
  3. We support your products and help you scale & improve them.
  4. We train and coach your organization to do what we do.

We build high-performance, agile teams that stick together from project to project. On average, we're 2-3x faster than inexperienced product teams. Each team has the skills makeup of a talented product startup, with the added benefit that they've been through it before together.

Who we work with

We tend to work on products that emphasize:

  • Reach - The problem you're solving can potentially impact large numbers of people directly or indirectly.
  • Impact - People care about the problem, and it has a significant impact on their lives.
  • Innovation - There is something new and novel in the approach being taken.
  • Agility - There an emphasis on speed, relentless iteration and absolute focus on users.
  • Experience - Users are given an unforgettable experience when interacting with the product.

Sometimes we make trade-offs - we're okay with products that deeply impact a small number of people. But these criteria guide our client selection and help us stay focused as a company.
