What We Do

Friday, Mar 14, 2014

Myplanet was founded in 2009 by a group of geeks hell-bent on changing the world.

Humbly beginning with five guys stuffed in a 500 square foot office in downtown Toronto, our team has grown to over 90 today (and rising!). Firm believers in the power of great user experiences to create meaning in people’s lives, outspoken champions of open source, practitioners of agile from top to bottom - these are but a few characteristics of the Myplaneteer. We’ve applied our philosophy at some of the biggest companies in the world and the most interesting nonprofits. But hey, we’re not bragging - we just love what we do!

Building an innovative, disruptive web and mobile application is hard

Just ask some of these guys we’ve worked with…

Most enterprises use a business- or IT-driven inside-out approach. For disruptive or innovative products, we think it’s risky.

We believe the right product should be user experience (UX) driven. It’s outside in, not inside out. The outside-in approach acknowledges that there is a gap between declared vs. revealed needs.

Declared needs create assumptions. If left unvalidated, the wrong product gets built. The goal of an outside-in approach is to test assumptions and reveal validated problems, solutions, and architecture.

Utility

  • Assumption: A problem exists and we need to solve it.
  • Validation: Does the problem really exist? Is there a demand for the soltuion?

Usability

  • Assumption: Our solution to the problem is the most effective way.
  • Validation: How many potential solutions have you tested? How does it comapre to the competition?

Feasability

  • Assumption: We've built the right architeture for our solution.
  • Validation: Do you have a clear sense of the desired user experience? Do you have the right skill sets to built it?

How do we build the right products outside-in and fast?

We Are Relentless in Uncovering User Truth

That means we're kind of obsessed with understanding what users really want. We use an immersive user validation process that is a combination of user-centered design thinking and lean validation processes, designed to help organizations to avoid the trap of in-the-box-thinking and instead, encourage the exploration of new opportunities of thought to get it right the first time.

  1. User-Centered Design: Because we're always thinking about the user, we are seriously disciplined in our approach of how we uncover, prioritize and solve for actual user needs. Attention to detail matters. In each detail lies the potential to delight, to inspire, and to impact. That, to us, is all the meaning in the world. They win. You win. We all win
  2. Lean Validation Approaches: We believe that the leaner we are in how we go about our methods of understanding the user, makes us better by focusing through a customer-lens framework, that validates any internal business assumptions and product hypotheses . We do this very well

Rapid Product Development

We're pretty fast. The way we operate and set up our teams allows us to deliver more rapidly without compromising quality. Enterprises today, more than ever face more pressure to deliver timely, on-target products and an increasingly need for speed to-market in order to compete in a world of changing innovations.

  1. Agile Team Structure Our cross-disciplinary teams work in super agile fashion, reducing inefficiencies across the board and allowing us to be quickly adaptable when business needs change. We love and trust agile thinking - we live and breathe these principles daily in how we work together and with our customers.
  2. Streamlined Delivery We like to automate certain processes in our development phases. This means we can focus on improving product quality, respond faster to changes all while drastically reducing cycle time from code to deployment. Businesses really like this about us.

We believe our customers and their teams, need to feel enabled and empowered when they work with us. Being highly flexible on how customers want us to engage with them and their current team structures is something we highly value. Today, organizations are not only in need of augmented expertise in just services, but also require repeatable, and sustainable practices of these investments for the future. We believe enterprises cannot be empowered without equally empowered people.

  1. Flexible Engagement Models: We've got multiple ways of how we can work with organizations. This works well because we can seamlessly integrate at any stage of their product and service lifecycle. (Accelerator, Shoulder-to-Shoulder, Joint Venture). Our customers highly value our flexibility on the various ways we can fit into their existing world and needs.
  2. Inclusive Collaboration: Our clients get involved. They love our direct and collaborative ways we work together that support better decision making. This is core to our agile thinking on our belief we should work together as a collective.
  3. Enablement: Ensuring your teams are well equipped and strongly knowledged in new capabilities is a key element in how we empower organizational teams and stretch their investments further. Capacity and capability building that supports or enhances enterprise teams is an important part of driving repeatable and ongoing success.
