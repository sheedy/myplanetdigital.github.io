The Lassonde School of Engineering

Lassonde cultivates a dynamic, evolutionary approach to modern education; empowering creative minds with a mix of social conscience and entrepurneship to infuse not only within their education, but to encorporate these principles into their every day lives. Myplanet was the force that propelled this vision to fruitation.

Visit Website

Developing the intricate link between creativity and engineering.

With the growth of the Lassonce School of Engineering within the York University Community, Myplanet was excited about the oppourtunity to play a key role in not only designing the web presence, but also in building the brand that will represent the school for years to come.

The site needed to be flexible enough to fully represent each department within the Lassonde School of Engineering, while maintaining the main brand of the school.

Beginning with in-depth user and stakeholder research, the Myplanet team was tasked to understand the key needs of the primary users of the site. Building personas for prospective students, current students, outside community members and staff/faculty, the user research team was able to develop a thorough understanding of the site’s key users.

Telling the Lassonde story.

The main goal of the new Lassonde website was to tell a story; to challenge the perception of engineering and education by focusing on creative thinking. Every student is a citizen of the Renassance Engineers™ movement. Creating a sense of community for students to connect, learn and engage across multiple disciplines and perspectives was important for demonstrating why Lassonde is so unique.
