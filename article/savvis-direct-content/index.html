Savvis Direct

For the past year Myplanet has been a primary design and development partner for a key strategic initiative of CenturyLink, the third largest telecommunications company in North America.

After CenturyLink's recent purchase of Savvis, a Gartner's Magic Quadrant leader in Enterprise cloud solutions, Myplanet has assisted in taking the Savvis offering and building a direct-to-SMB marketplace, management console and assisted with go-to-market strategy.

Myplanet teams have directly augmented Savvis’ internal teams and resources in order to accelerate the pace at which products could be delivered to market.

Collaboratively with Acquia, Myplanet helped design and build a highly configurable eCommerce solution for Savvis Direct.

Built on Drupal and Drupal Commerce, the solution offers a competitive platform to companies such as Rackspace and Amazon.

Myplanet has been closely collaborating with the leadership team at Savvis Direct in a strategic capacity to drive not only product development but long-term technology roadmap planning. Savvis Direct is a top strategic priority for CenturyLink and Myplanet has played an integral role in bringing solutions to market.
