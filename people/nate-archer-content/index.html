Who I am

Born in Toronto, I studied industrial design in my hometown as well as in Amsterdam. I worked in Milan before coming back to Toronto to join Myplanet in 2011. Some know me to have a strange sense of humour, drink black coffee and I ride my bike in the winter.

What I do

I am a thinker and doer who believes in designing the change you wish to see in the world. I spend my days conducting design research, synthesizing findings and designing interactions for great digital products. I am passionate about solving wicked problems and advocating for user centered design.
