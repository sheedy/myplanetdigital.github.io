Who I am

As a creative lead, I find myself passionate about design in all shapes and sizes from visual-interactive, interaction design to user research. I am a geek for gaining those nuggets of user insights that transforms a good product into one that carries deep impact in its final execution. When not being a full-time creative, I’m also a part-time traveller, a self-proclaimed film critic and an avid linguaphile.

What I do

My mission at Myplanet is to create digital products with the greatest impact possible. I value impact in three ways: User Impact, Creative Impact and Community Impact. I pride myself on doing work that enriches the lives of users, while also enhancing the mastery of my craft and contributing towards a more sustainable industry at large.
