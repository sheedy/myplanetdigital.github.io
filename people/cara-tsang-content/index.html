Who I am

I have a boundless curiosity about what makes people tick. This interest led me deep into the world of Cognitive Psychology, where I spent years fiddling with eye-trackers and testing human guinea pigs young and old to discover how they understand language.

After dealing with one (dozen...) too many frustrating UI's, I was inspired to leave the ivory towers and use my research powers for the greater good: To help create products that result in intuitive, elegant, and engaging user experiences.

The Maillard Reaction, the interwebs, pop jazz, improv ... these are a few of my favourite things. I also enjoys long walks on the beach, but only if they lead to cheese or bacon.

What I do

I try to get inside people's heads! I dig into users' beliefs, attitudes, needs, and goals, and then use that to figure out how to build the best thing for them. And then I show that thing to more users, and see if it breaks. Lather, rinse, repeat, love.

I also make a lot of terrible puns, attend a lot of office Fiestas, and drink an entirely reasonable amount of beer on Beer Fridays.
