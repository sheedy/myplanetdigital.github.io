Who I am

I'm Charlie. I love pushing boundaries and making new things. I'm a big fan of technology, and using it to empower people. I have a degree in Knowledge Integration and Computer Science from the University of Waterloo, where my research focused on interactive digital design. When I'm not busy working on new product concepts, you can usually find me gallivanting around a far-flung continent, or waist-deep in backcountry powder on my snowboard.

What I do

Here at Myplanet, I'm currently tasked with product strategy and execution for Relay Robin (www.relayrobin.com), our new suite of video-conferencing tools. I work closely with our carrier partners, end-users, designers, and developers to make our products the best they can possibly be. I have also worked as an agile Product Owner on our prototyping team, creating functional prototypes of next-generation cloud data center management tools.
