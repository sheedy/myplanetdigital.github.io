Who I am

My passion is human performance and applying leading methods to bring people closer together and to their peak potential. As a child I strived to become Indiana Jones and am a full-on adventurer at heart. I've trekked almost every country in Asia and spend my free time on retreat in the Himalaya's. I've been bitten by monkey's, fallen off of cliffs and have growled back at a wild Tiger during a harrowing jungle trek...

After extensive expeditions throughout the Far East (and after a bit of time launching three of my own start-ups, working for one of the worlds top PR & Marketing firms and studying Business Management and Organizational Studies at Western University), I joined Myplanet 3 1/2 years ago as Team and Talent Development Lead.

What I do

I am a certified emotional intelligence coach and team collaboration specialist. I'm also Senior Faculty and Co-ordinator for the Mind@Work and Mindful Leadership programs at University of Toronto where I teach mindfulness-based emotional intelligence for performance and team collaboration. I'm founder of University Health Networks Mindfulness for Leadership and Resiliency program serving 10,000+ healthcare professionals and at the end of it all, simply love the culture we've created at Myplanet!
