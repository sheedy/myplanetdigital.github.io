Who I am

Born in Jamaica, I have worked in the West Indies in print and advertising. In 2009, I moved to Toronto to study Graphic Design at OCAD U. Since graduating from the Myplanet Fellowship program in early 2013, I have been living and breathing Myplanet.

What I do

Most of my days are spent working in print, packaging and branding. When I'm not doing that, I am critiquing and learning from my peers.
