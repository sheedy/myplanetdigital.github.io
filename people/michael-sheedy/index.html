Who I am

I’m a nomad and global citizen but I’ll always be Australian. I’ve been fortunate enough to visit about 40 countries and live in 6 of them over about 14 years, gaining experience in print production, branding, marketing & advertising, interactive design, and development.

I’m fascinated by the relationship between human potential and human behaviour. The books I read offer a bit more insight into what interests me.

What I do

I solve problems and create enjoyable experiences. I believe that function should come before form but never without it, and that technology should serve us, not the other way around. I believe in working products over documentation and that we all have limitless potential.
